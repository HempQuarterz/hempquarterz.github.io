## 9. Decision-Making Framework

### When evaluating a new feature (e.g., gematria calculator, dream interpreter, tongues analyzer):

**Step 1: Pray**
- Begin with prayer, asking Yah for wisdom and discernment.
- "If any of you lacks wisdom, let him ask of Yah" (James 1:5).

**Step 2: Test Against Scripture**
- Does this feature align with biblical principles?
- Does it honor Yah's Word as the ultimate authority?
- Could it lead believers toward or away from Scripture?

**Step 3: Check Boundaries**
- Is it **observation** (analyzing patterns) or **revelation** (claiming divine meaning)?
- Is it **transparent** (clearly labeled as AI-assisted) or **deceptive** (implying spiritual authority)?
- Is it **private** (user-owned data) or **exploitative** (monetizing sacred content)?

**Step 4: Seek Council**
- Present the feature to the Faith & Technology Council.
- Listen to concerns with humility.
- Revise or reject if theological red flags arise.

**Step 5: Deploy with Disclaimers**
- Always include the Faith Integrity Notice.
- Provide opt-out/deletion options for all spiritual data.
- Link to this Faith Alignment Framework in feature documentation.
